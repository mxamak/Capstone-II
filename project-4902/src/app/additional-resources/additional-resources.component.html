University of North Texas


Additional Resources

A curated list of useful tools, support services, and platforms to help you through every step of your research journey at UNT and beyond.

UNT Service

UNT Libraries

Access databases, e-journals, citation tools, and subject-specific research guides.

Ideal starting point for literature reviews, data sourcing, or citation management across disciplines.

UNT Service

Office of Research & Innovation

Find funding opportunities, proposal assistance, and guidance on research policies.

For faculty and students pursuing grants or navigating compliance and ethics.

UNT Service

Graduate Research Resources

Resources for theses, dissertations, and graduate-level research processes.

Explore formatting templates, deadlines, and funding options tailored for grad students.

UNT Program

Undergraduate Research Programs

Fellowships, research mentorship, and showcase opportunities for undergrads.

Perfect for students seeking hands-on research experience and presentation skills.

Tech Space

UNT Makerspace

Access 3D printers, laser cutters, and design software in the Willis Library innovation hub.

Great for prototyping, product testing, and interdisciplinary research projects.

Guidelines

Research Integrity & Ethics

Learn best practices for data handling, publication, and ethical research involving human subjects.

A must-read before submitting IRB applications or initiating human-centered studies.

External Guide

How to Do Research – MIT OpenCourseWare

Learn how to form research questions, conduct literature reviews, and present findings.

Ideal for beginners in research or anyone needing a refresher from top-tier educators.

Search Tool

Google Scholar

Discover scholarly articles, theses, books, and conference papers across disciplines.

Start your research with a global academic search engine connected to citation tracking.

Tool

Zotero – Research Organization Tool

Collect, organize, cite, and share research sources with a powerful browser extension.

Keeps your workflow organized and helps you generate citations automatically.

Self-Assessment

URSSA – Undergraduate Research Skills Self-Assessment

Reflect on your own growth and learning as a researcher through guided questions.

Useful for undergrads preparing for conferences, fellowships, or graduate school.
