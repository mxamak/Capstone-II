How It Works

Research Finder makes it easy for students and researchers to connect and collaborate. Here's how to get started:

1.

Sign up or log in

Create an account or log in to access research opportunities and connect with others.

2.

Browse or create research opportunities

Explore available projects or post your own to invite collaborators.

3.

Connect with researchers or participants

Reach out, express your interest, and start meaningful conversations around research.

4.

Contribute and collaborate!

Join a research team, share your skills, and be part of the discovery process.
