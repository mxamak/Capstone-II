Who We Are

Research Finder is your gateway to collaborative research. We connect passionate students with researchers to make innovation more accessible, one project at a time.

Our Mission

We believe research should be accessible to all. Our mission is to democratize discovery by giving students a direct pathway into research teams and helping researchers find motivated contributors across disciplines.
